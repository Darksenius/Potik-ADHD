Potik ("Flow" in Ukrainian) is not yet another to-do list — it is an external executive-function support system for people with ADHD. Instead of asking "what should I do?", it answers "what is best right now?".

Works fully offline. All data stays on your device. No ads, no trackers, no accounts.

<b>Three pillars:</b>
• <b>Day zones</b> — the day is split into colored time zones (Sleep / Morning / Work / Lunch / Evening…); the UI changes color and tasks are ranked by the current zone
• <b>Hyperfocus tracking</b> — log where your attention actually is, with switch times and durations
• <b>Impulse capture</b> — caught a thought? Dump it into the notebook and get back to what you were doing

<b>Features:</b>
• Many task types: simple, checklists, counters, alarms, scheduled, habits, Pomodoro, kids' tasks
• Week builder — 5/2, 2/2, 3/3 and custom schedules, day templates
• Persistent notification with the current zone and tasks — with ✓ / snooze / note actions
• Quick notes with folders, free-form notebook, "someday" ideas
• Routine, energy and water — gentle tracking without self-criticism
• XP, levels, day streaks, achievements — a short distance between action and reward
• JSON export/import, automatic on-disk backup, Markdown notes export

The interface is currently in Ukrainian only.
